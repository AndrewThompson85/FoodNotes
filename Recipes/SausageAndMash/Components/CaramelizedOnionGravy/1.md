### Caramelized Onion Gravy

#### Ingredients

| Name | Quantity | Percent | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
|Beef Stock|450 grams|100%| Packet is fine, but use low sodium|
|Red Onion| 320 grams|71%| Peeled and thinly sliced with mandolin|
|Brown Onion| 220 grams|49%| Peeled and thinly sliced with mandolin|
|Bicarbonate of soda|5.4 grams|1.2%||



#### Method

1. Pressure cook for 1 hour. Allow pressure to release naturally.
 - Red Onion
 - Brown Onion
 - Bicarbonate of soda
 - Water (Enough to cover onions - c. 900g)


2. Reduce by approximately half
3. Add the beef stock.
4. Pressure cook for 1 hour. Allow pressure to release naturally.
5. Strain liquid and discard onions.
6. Reduce gently until sauce reaches desired thickness.
7. Season to taste.

#### Yield

- 2 small portions.

#### Notes


- Has slightly bitter note.

#### What would I change

- Reduce end sauce a bit more slowly (Maybe the sauce burned a little and made it bitter)
- Accurately measure yield
- Accurately measure seasoning for better indication.
