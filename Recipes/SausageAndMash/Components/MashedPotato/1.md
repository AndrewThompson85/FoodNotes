### Mashed Potatoes

#### Ingredients

| Name | Quantity | Percent | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
|Camel Potatoes|260g|100%|Peeled, sliced thick and washed|
|Unsalted Butter|65g|25%|Cut into pieces|
|Whole Milk|30g|11.5%||
|Garlic|10g|3.8%|Minced|



#### Method

1. Seal all ingredients in bag
2. Cook sous vide at 90c for 1 hour
3. Mash whilst inside bag
4. Optionally, pass potatoes through a potato ricer to improve texture.

#### Yield

1 Large serving.

#### Notes

I was very happy with the result. A reasonable amount of mashing inside of the bag
was required to mix the liquids and solids.

#### What would I change

- I would maybe tweak the amount of whole milk added.
- More accurately measure yield.
