### Tandoori Chicken

#### Ingredients

| Name | Quantity | Percent | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
|Chicken Leg quarter|1058g|||
|**For the brine**|
|Water|300g|28%||
|Salt|18g|2%|6% of the mass of water used|
|Tandoori Powder|15g|2%||
|**For the marinade**|
|Tandoori Powder|15g|2%||
|Garlic & Ginger Paste|60g|6%||
|Full fat natural yoghurt|100g|11%||

#### Preparation

1. Pre heat oven to highest value, turn on the broiler if possible. Heat up a heavy tray. High temperature is crucial

#### Method

1. Prepare the brine
 - Mix all brine ingredients

2. Inject as much brine as you can into the chicken...injections at 1" intervals
3. Prepare the marinade
 - Mix all marinade ingredients
4. Marinade chicken for at least 8 hours
5. Cook chicken at top of oven at highest possible temperature for approx. 30 mins

#### Notes
> ##### Iteration 1
> ##### Tasting
> Chicken was juicy but not completely tender. It tasted a little bit under seasoned.
> Color was nice. I would prefer some additional smoky notes on the outside.
>
>#### What would I change
>
> Cook for slightly longer time to yield a more tender chicken texture.
> This should also yield a slightly smokier exterior.
> I may introduce a small amount of smoked paprika into the marinade to help with the smokiness, and season the marinade as well.
