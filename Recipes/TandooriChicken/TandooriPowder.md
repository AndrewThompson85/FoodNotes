### Tandoori Powder

#### Ingredients

| Name | Quantity | Percent | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
|Cayenne Pepper|22g|100%||
|Whole Coriander|15g|68%||
|Whole Cumin|15g|68%||
|Black Peppercorn|5g|23%||
|Fenugreek Seed|5g|23%||
|Green Cardamom Seed|3g|14%||
|Fennel Seed|3g|14%||
|Mace blade|3g|14%||
|Food Coloring|6.2g|28%||


#### Method

1. Roast whole spices at 100 degrees for 1 hour
2. Turn heat up to 140 degrees for 30 minutes
3. Allow whole spices to cool
4. Grind spices to a Powder
5. Mix in Cayenne pepper and food coloring

#### Yield
- Approx. 77g
